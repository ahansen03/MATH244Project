This MATH244: Intermediate Data Science final project aims to analyze the characteristics that contribute to a song's popularity and charting success. Utilizing a dataset of the top 100 songs for each year from 2010-2019, we will perform regression analysis on various Spotify track statistics (BPM, loudness, danceability, energy, valence, etc.) to predict a song's chart position or overall popularity score. This analysis will then be compared against a master dataset of overall Spotify track averages. By comparing the characteristics of charted songs with the overall Spotify song statistics, our goal is to identify specific track characteristics that are correlated with making a song break into the top charts and generally become a mainstream success. We will also be looking at trends over the decade, observing what kinds of music resonated most with users in each year. We expect this to change drastically, as the popular music from the early 2010s seems considerably different from that from the late 2010s.
